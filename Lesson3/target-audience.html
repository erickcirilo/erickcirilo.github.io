Target Audience

Target Audience People who wants to plan activities and events outsite of their houses as well as those professionals who depends on good weather to perform their work. Additionally, anyone interested in checking the weather conditions.

  4. I have a family activity in the park, how will the weather be?
  5. Should I go out with an umbrella today?
