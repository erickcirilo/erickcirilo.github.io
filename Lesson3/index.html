Weather Site Purpose

Weather warnings are important forecasts because they are used to protect life and property. Nowadays I think it is very difficult to find someone who is not interested in how the weather will be during the day and the week. With the speed with which things move, it is valuable to have as a reference a reliable and safe site with the greatest precision in weather predictions.
